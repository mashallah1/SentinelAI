01 — Problem Statement

DeFi risk is fast, silent, and expensive

💀
Depeg Events Strike Fast
UST, USDD, and multiple other stablecoins collapsed in hours. By the time most users noticed, liquidity had already dried up. Reactive monitoring isn't enough.
🕳️
Fragmented Chain Visibility
Assets span Mantle, Ethereum, Arbitrum and more. No single tool gives a unified, real-time risk picture across chains simultaneously.
⏱️
Manual Response is Too Slow
DAOs and treasuries can't monitor positions 24/7. Risk events don't wait for a governance vote or a human approving a Gnosis Safe transaction.
02 — Solution

Detect. Alert. Reroute.

STEP 01
⬡ Ingest
Continuously pulls on-chain data: liquidity depth, peg deviation, collateral ratios, smart contract activity, and wallet flows across Mantle and connected chains.
STEP 02
⬡ Score
AI models assign real-time Risk Scores (0–100) per asset. Models trained on historical depeg patterns, liquidity crises, and protocol exploits.
STEP 03
⬡ Alert
When thresholds are crossed, SentinelAI fires push notifications and surfaces a pre-built rerouting recommendation — no manual analysis needed.
STEP 04
⬡ Execute
User reviews the suggested action and approves with one click. Mantle smart contracts handle atomic execution — swap, move, or hedge in a single transaction.

04 — Target Users

Built for everyone with exposure

⚡ DeFi Power Users & Whales
High-value individual positions across multiple protocols. Too much at stake to rely on manual monitoring. Need fast, confident alerts with immediate 1-click action.
→ Value: Preserve portfolio value during black swan events. React in seconds, not hours.
🏦 DAOs & Protocol Treasuries
Mantle DAO and peer protocols holding large stablecoin or multi-asset positions. Governance cycles too slow for real-time risk. Need delegated, semi-automatic safeguards.
→ Value: Set risk thresholds once. SentinelAI monitors 24/7, surfaces human-approved actions instantly.
🔁 Protocols Managing Liquidity
DEXes, lending protocols, and yield vaults on Mantle that need to maintain healthy liquidity ratios and avoid exposure concentration during volatile markets.
→ Value: Programmatic risk signals embedded directly into protocol risk management workflows.
05 — Technical Architecture

How it's built

Layer 1 — Data
On-Chain Indexer
Indexes Mantle + Ethereum + Arbitrum blocks in real-time. Tracks pool reserves, stablecoin peg deviations, collateral ratios, and wallet flow anomalies.
Layer 2 — Intelligence
AI Risk Models
LSTM + gradient boosting ensemble trained on historical crisis data. Outputs a continuous 0–100 Risk Score per asset with labeled risk factors.
Layer 3 — Action
Rerouting Engine
Pre-computed optimal routes using Mantle DEX liquidity data. Presented to user for 1-click approval, then executed atomically via Mantle smart contract.
Layer 4 — Interface
Dashboard + Alerts
Web dashboard with real-time risk feed. Push notifications via Telegram / email / webhook. API available for protocol integrations and governance tooling.
